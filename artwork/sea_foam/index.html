Sea Foam

Sea Foam is an installation built by Mollie Bryan for which I was the lighting designer. It's a series of lights surrounded by filters with colors that shift and shimmer in waves. Full arduino source code for this lighting design is available on GitHub.

Vicki Fehl also posted a good photo of this installation to Instagram
