Artwork

Inflection

Inflection was a large wood and rope bridge designed and produced by Trevor Schrock, supported by the Tautology Arts Collective as an honorarium art project for Burning Man in 2015. I produced this project's lighting and power systems.

Photography

Some of my favorite thigns to have come out of my cameras over the years.

Rebirth

Rebirth is an LED lightboard I built with John Windberg displaying a modified version of an artificial life algorithm. It's one of the simplest models of a self-reproducing Turing machine, and in this form the emergence lives indefinitely based on random co-occurrences.

Sea Foam

Sea Foam is an installation built by Mollie Bryan for which I was the lighting designer. It's a series of lights surrounded by filters with colors that shift and shimmer in waves.

Synaesthesia Guitar

This is a guitar that I've modified to simulate my form of synaesthesia. The lights along the ourside display the colors I see from the music I play on it.

The Silk Road

The Silk Road was a Burning Man honorarium art installation led by Julia Logan Trimarco. I did the the structural engineering and some carpentry, designed and built the nighttime lighting electronics and power grid, then worked with a stellar team to install it beside the Temple of Grace at Burning Man 2014.
